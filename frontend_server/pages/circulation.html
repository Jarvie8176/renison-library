Prior to the completion of the EASC project in August 2015, circulation was conducted manually through borrowing cards.

Currently, all books have a corresponding digital record that is collected within a Readerware database. Each item’s digital record is identified by a unique barcode number (“Call Number" in Readerware).

The searching, loaning, and returning of items is done through the Readerware software. Patrons may conduct a search of the Readerware database through this online portal, or at a self-serve terminal within the Lusi Wong library.
