Each book in the collection is marked with a colored dot to indicate the cultural group:

  • Yellow for Korean

  • Red for Chinese

  • Green for Japanese

The books are shelved together in their respective cultural groups.

Every book is organized by Dewey decimal number within its cultural group. Because of the limitations of the original Dewey decimal system, we use a modified version that was developed in-house to meet the needs of the East Asian collection.

Following the Dewey number, a three-character Cutter code indicates the authorâ€™s surname.

Finally, each book is affixed with a barcode that serves as a unique identifier and makes possible automated scanning.
